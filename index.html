About Me

Transforming nature’s resources through science

My name is Rediat Terefe, born and raised in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. I hold both Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Chemical Engineering (Process Stream) from Jimma University, Jimma Institute of Technology, Jimma, Ethiopia. Currently, I am pursuing my Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee (IIT Roorkee), with a research focus on the conversion of biomass into fuel intermediates using heterogeneous catalysis, and upgrading the intermediates, particularly targeting furfural and Furfuryl alcohol. My academic and research interests include catalyst design and preparation, designing of experiments, molecular-level studies using DFT, and reaction parameters optimization, all driven by a strong passion for advancing sustainable chemical processes.
